About Us

Our Story

Statewide Cabinets, Inc. is one of Central Florida's most trusted cabinetry design and installation studios. For over 25 years, we have been committed to meeting the needs of our customers, designing beautiful kitchens and baths as well as whole-house cabinetry. Our combination of terrific products with incredible design and remodeling services will help bring the design results you have been dreaming of.

Our Approach

At Statewide Cabinets, we believe in a collaborative approach. Our highly trained staff will work closely with you to understand your vision, lifestyle, and budget. From initial consultation to final installation, we are dedicated to providing exceptional service and craftsmanship, ensuring that your project is completed to your satisfaction.

Our Services

From concept to creation, Statewide Cabinets offers a complete range of custom cabinetry services to transform your home.

Cabinet Design

Our experienced designers will work with you to create custom cabinet designs tailored to your specific needs, style preferences, and budget. We'll help you select the perfect materials, finishes, and hardware to bring your vision to life.

Cabinet Installation

Our skilled installers will ensure your new cabinets are installed with precision and care. We'll handle every detail, from removing your old cabinets to ensuring a perfect fit and finish for your new ones.

Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling

Beyond cabinets, we offer comprehensive kitchen and bathroom remodeling services. From countertops and backsplashes to flooring and lighting, we'll coordinate every aspect of your project to create the space of your dreams.
